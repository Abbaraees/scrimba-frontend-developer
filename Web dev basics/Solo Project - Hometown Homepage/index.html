Visit Daura

Enjoy culture and tradition in this vibrant historic town located at the Nothern Nigeria.

Top three activities to do at Daura

Durbar Festival

The festival marks the end of Ramadan and also coincides with the Muslim festivities of Eid al-Adha and Eid al-Fitri.

Visit the Legendary Kusugu Well

The well is famous for its relation to the legend of the hero Bayajidda defeating the snake Sarki. The well and Bayajidda's supposed dagger are now a tourist attraction.
